Exchange with Mirela Kovac at the university's digital fabrication lab about CNC-milling translucent forms.

Hi Lena,

I've looked at your CAD files. The wall thickness you specified (4mm) is feasible in TGMA on our 3-axis CNC, but at that thickness the material won't diffuse the LEDs as well as you'd like. We tested a sample piece last week — individual LED hotspots are still visible through 4mm TGMA.

I'd suggest either increasing to 6-8mm (which changes the form significantly) or adding a layer of diffusion film on the interior surface before embedding the LEDs. We have some samples of Lumiveil D2 and D4 that might work.

Happy to schedule a test session next week if you're interested.

Best,
Mirela

This is very helpful. The diffusion film approach is elegant — it separates the structural and optical functions of the material. Scheduled a session for July 22.
