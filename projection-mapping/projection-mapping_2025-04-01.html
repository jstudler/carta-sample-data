--- title: Introduction — Projection Mapping date: 2025-04-01 topic: projection-mapping category: studio-work type: introduction ---

The second research strand investigates how programmed light patterns can be mapped onto three-dimensional sculptural surfaces. Two fundamentally different approaches are explored:

  1. Embedded LEDs — addressable LED strips and matrices cast in translucent resin or mounted inside hollow sculptural forms.
  2. Projected light — using short-throw projectors to paint light patterns directly onto opaque sculptural surfaces.

Each approach has distinct advantages and trade-offs in terms of resolution, color fidelity, portability, and the relationship between the light and the sculptural form. This strand aims to develop a practical understanding of when each technique is most effective and whether hybrid approaches are viable.
