--- title: Introduction — Sensor Integration date: 2025-03-10 topic: sensor-integration category: research type: introduction ---

The first strand of this research focuses on environmental sensing — the process of capturing data about the physical environment surrounding a sculpture and translating it into signals that can drive lighting behavior.

Initial questions:

  • Which environmental parameters are most perceptually relevant for gallery visitors? Temperature, humidity, ambient sound, proximity?
  • How should raw sensor data be filtered and smoothed to produce aesthetically meaningful control signals?
  • What is the minimum viable sensor array for a responsive light sculpture?

I begin with a survey of available sensor technologies and a series of bench tests to evaluate their reliability, latency, and noise characteristics in a typical gallery environment.
