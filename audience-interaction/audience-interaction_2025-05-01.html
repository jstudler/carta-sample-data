--- title: Introduction — Audience Interaction date: 2025-05-01 topic: audience-interaction category: research type: introduction ---

The third strand of research examines what happens when viewers encounter a responsive light sculpture. How do people behave around an object that seems to react to their presence? What interaction patterns emerge, and how do these patterns inform the design of future works?

This topic draws on methods from interaction design, phenomenology, and ethnographic observation. I plan to document audience behavior through a combination of:

  • Direct observation during exhibition openings and gallery hours
  • Brief semi-structured interviews with willing participants
  • Video documentation (with consent) of interaction sequences

The goal is not to optimize for engagement metrics but to understand the qualitative character of the viewer-sculpture relationship. What does it feel like to be in dialogue with an illuminated object?
