Dear Lena,

Thank you for your proposal for the autumn group show "Responsive Environments." We'd be very interested in including one of your kinetic light sculptures in the exhibition.

A few practical questions:

  • What are the spatial requirements? We could offer a 3x3m area in the main gallery or a 2x4m alcove space.
  • Does the work require complete darkness, or can it function in reduced ambient light?
  • What is the lead time for installation and calibration?

We've had good experiences with interactive works in the alcove — visitors tend to spend more time with pieces that feel somewhat separate from the main exhibition flow.

Looking forward to hearing from you.
